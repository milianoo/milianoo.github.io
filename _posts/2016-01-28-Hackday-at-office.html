Great idea, hard work and awesome team presenation, that's how we started new year in Nintex. Almost 30+ ideas registered by Ninsters (people who work at nintex) on how to improve products and workplace using technological solutions or ideas that helps business process.


30+ amazing ideas

Several ideas and concepts, has been posted to the hackathon board and people pitched the ideas in order to recruit for the development team. It is interesting to see how everyone of developers and testers are thinking about creative ways to make the office a better place to work.


Hard work, Eat More

Besides all the fun that we had during working with people from other teams and products and also enjoying all the great foods and snacks several times, this hackathon was a great opportuinity for ninsters to implement great solutions for our daily problems or to demonstrate creative ways on how to integrate Nintex products with other tools and services.

This all happened while we learn new things and trying new ways to hack our ideas and build amazing tools.
